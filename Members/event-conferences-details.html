Event Title: Ceramica West Africa
Date: 21 May 2021
Landmark Centre, Lagos, Nigeria
Timings
10:00 AM - 05:00 PM (GMT+1)
Price of ticket

Speakers

Description
Annually organized launch event 2nd CERAMICA Expo will bring together the sector leader companies at Lagos Landmark Event Centre on 19-21 May 2021!
